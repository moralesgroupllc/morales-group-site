Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 18, 2026

1. Overview

Morales Group LLC is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you visit our website, how we use it, and how we keep it safe. By using this site, you agree to the practices described below.

2. Information We Collect

We only collect information you voluntarily provide to us. This may include:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number (if provided)
  • The content of any message or inquiry you submit through our contact form

We do not collect any information automatically, and we do not use tracking cookies or analytics tools at this time.

3. How We Use Your Information

Any information you submit is used solely to respond to your inquiry or to follow up regarding the services you expressed interest in. We will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing or any other purpose.

This website does not use cookies. If that changes in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly and clearly communicated.

5. External Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites such as Zillow or other platforms. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those external sites. We encourage you to review their policies before submitting any personal information.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information you share with us. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we treat your information with care and discretion.

7. Your Rights

You may contact us at any time to request that we delete any personal information you have submitted to us. We will honor such requests promptly.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or services. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your information, please reach out to us at info@themoralesgroupllc.com.
